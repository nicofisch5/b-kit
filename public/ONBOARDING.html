What is B-Strack?

B-Strack is a Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to help coaches, statisticians, and team staff track basketball game statistics in real-time. Built with modern web technologies, it works seamlessly on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

Key Features

  • Real-time Statistics Tracking: Record all basketball stats including points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks, fouls, and turnovers
  • Quarter Management: Track statistics by quarter (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4) and overtime periods
  • Player Management: Manage up to 12 players with customizable names and jersey numbers
  • Automatic Score Calculation: Home team score updates automatically based on recorded field goals and free throws
  • Offline Capability: Works without internet connection once installed
  • Auto-Save: Data saved automatically every 30 seconds to prevent loss
  • Export Options: Export game data as JSON for backup or analysis

Getting Started

1 Access the App

Scan the QR code above or visit the app URL in your mobile browser. For best experience, use Chrome, Safari, or Edge.

2 Install as PWA (Recommended)

When prompted, tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install". This allows the app to work offline and load faster.

  • iOS Safari: Tap the share icon → "Add to Home Screen"
  • Android Chrome: Tap the menu (⋮) → "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
3 Customize Your Team

Before the game starts, tap "Edit Players" to customize player names and jersey numbers for your team.

4 Start Tracking

You're ready to go! Click any stat button, select the player, and watch the stats update in real-time.

How to Record Statistics

Basic Workflow

  1. Select the Quarter: Choose Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4, or + OT
  2. Click a Stat Button: Examples: 2PT Made Assist Def Reb
  3. Choose the Player: A modal will appear - tap the player who performed the action
  4. Confirm: The stat is recorded and displays update automatically
Tip: For field goals (2PT/3PT) and free throws, you can also select an assist after choosing the scorer!

Understanding the Interface

Score Display (Top)

Shows the current home team score (auto-calculated) and opposition score (manually entered)

Quarter Selector

Switch between quarters to view or record stats for different periods. The active quarter is highlighted.

Player Roster (Left/Top)

Displays all players with their current fouls and points. Players with 5 fouls are highlighted in red.

Stats Control Panel (Center/Bottom)

Grid of buttons for recording different basketball statistics:

  • Green buttons: Made shots (2PT Made, 3PT Made, FT Made)
  • Red buttons: Missed shots (2PT Miss, 3PT Miss, FT Miss)
  • Blue buttons: Other actions (Rebounds, Assists, Steals, Blocks, Fouls, Turnovers)

Action Bar (Bottom)

Contains important game management functions:

  • Undo: Reverse the last recorded action
  • Box Score: View comprehensive game statistics
  • Quarter Logs: See all events recorded in the current quarter
  • Edit Players: Modify player information
  • Export: Download game data as JSON
  • New Game: Start fresh (with confirmation)

Best Practices

Before the Game:
  • Edit player names and jersey numbers
  • Enter the opposition team name
  • Ensure your device is charged or connected to power
  • Test the app to familiarize yourself with the interface
During the Game:
  • Record stats as they happen - don't wait
  • Use the Undo button if you make a mistake
  • Update the opposition score periodically
  • Switch quarters at the end of each period
  • Keep your device on and the app in the foreground
After the Game:
  • Review the Box Score for complete statistics
  • Export the game data for your records
  • Share screenshots or export files with your team
  • Start a new game when ready for the next match

Troubleshooting

App not loading?

Check your internet connection. After the first load, the app works offline.

Data disappeared?

Data is stored locally. If you cleared browser data or switched devices, data may be lost. Export regularly!

Can't install as PWA?

Ensure you're using HTTPS (not HTTP) and that your browser supports PWAs. Try Chrome or Safari.

Screen too small?

The app is responsive. Try rotating your device to landscape mode or zooming out.

Undo not working?

Undo only works for the most recent action. Once you record a new stat, the previous undo is lost.
